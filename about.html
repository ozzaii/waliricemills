Our History

Salman Rice Mill was founded in 1980 by Salman Ahmad, a visionary farmer with a passion for quality rice. What started as a small family-owned mill has grown into one of the leading rice producers in Sheikhpura, Punjab.

Over the past four decades, we've remained committed to our founding principles: uncompromising quality, sustainable farming practices, and support for our local community. Today, our state-of-the-art facility combines traditional knowledge with modern technology to produce some of the finest rice in Pakistan.

Our Mission

At Salman Rice Mill, our mission is to deliver premium quality rice to tables around the world while supporting sustainable agriculture and empowering local farmers. We strive to preserve the rich traditions of Pakistani rice cultivation while embracing innovation to meet the evolving needs of our customers.

Our Values

  • Quality: We are committed to producing rice of the highest quality, from seed selection to packaging.
  • Sustainability: We implement eco-friendly practices to minimize our environmental impact and ensure long term viability.
  • Community: We support local farmers and contribute to the economic development of Sheikhpura.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek ways to improve our processes and products through technology and research.
  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and respect for all stakeholders.
