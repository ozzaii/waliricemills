Our Quality Certifications

ISO 22000:2018

This certification ensures that our food safety management system meets international standards, guaranteeing the safety of our rice products throughout the entire supply chain.

HACCP

Our HACCP certification demonstrates our commitment to identifying and controlling potential food safety hazards in our rice production process.

Organic Certification

This certification applies to our organic rice products, ensuring they are grown and processed according to strict organic standards without the use of synthetic pesticides or fertilizers.

Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP)

Our GMP certification ensures that our rice products are consistently produced and controlled according to quality standards.

Our Certification Process

At Salman Rice Mill, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety. Our certification process involves:

  • Regular audits by independent certification bodies
  • Continuous staff training on quality and safety procedures
  • Implementing and maintaining robust management systems
  • Ongoing monitoring and improvement of our processes

Our Commitment to Quality

These certifications are more than just documents to us. They represent our ongoing commitment to providing our customers with the highest quality rice products. We continuously strive to improve our processes and maintain the trust our customers place in us.
