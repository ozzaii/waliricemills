Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our rice and services

What types of rice do you produce?

We produce a variety of high-quality rice, including:

  • Premium Long Grain Rice
  • Aromatic Basmati Rice
  • Whole Grain Brown Rice
  • Parboiled Rice
  • Jasmine Rice

Each type is carefully cultivated and processed to meet our high standards of quality.

Where is your rice grown?

Our rice is primarily grown in the fertile fields of Sheikhpura, Punjab, Pakistan. We work closely with local farmers to ensure sustainable and high-quality cultivation practices.

Are your products organic?

While not all of our products are certified organic, we do offer a line of organic rice. All our products, organic or conventional, are grown using sustainable farming practices with minimal use of chemical inputs. We prioritize environmental stewardship in all our operations.

How do you ensure the quality of your rice?

We maintain strict quality control measures throughout our production process:

  • Careful selection of seeds and monitoring of crops during growth
  • State-of-the-art milling and processing facilities
  • Regular testing for purity, consistency, and nutritional value
  • Adherence to international food safety standards
  • Continuous training of our staff in best practices

Do you offer bulk purchasing options?

Yes, we offer bulk purchasing options for businesses, restaurants, and large-scale consumers. Please contact our sales team for more information on bulk orders and pricing.

How should I store your rice?

For optimal freshness and quality, store our rice in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Once opened, transfer the rice to an airtight container. Properly stored, our rice can maintain its quality for up to two years from the date of purchase.

Do you export your rice internationally?

Yes, we export our rice to various countries around the world. We comply with international export regulations and can provide necessary documentation for smooth customs clearance. Contact our export department for specific information about international orders.

What sustainable practices do you implement?

Sustainability is a core value at Salman Rice Mill. Some of our key practices include:

  • Water conservation techniques in our fields
  • Use of rice husks as biofuel in our milling process
  • Minimizing waste through efficient processing
  • Supporting local farmers with fair trade practices
  • Investing in renewable energy sources for our facilities

How can I become a distributor for Salman Rice Mill?

We're always looking for reliable partners to help distribute our products. If you're interested in becoming a distributor, please send us a detailed inquiry through our contact form or email our business development team at partners@salmanricemill.com. We'll review your application and get back to you with further information.
