Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our rice and services

What types of rice do you produce?

We produce a variety of rice types including Basmati, Long Grain, and Brown Rice. Each type is available in different grades to suit various culinary needs.

How do you ensure the quality of your rice?

We have a rigorous quality control process that includes careful seed selection, controlled growing conditions, and state-of-the-art milling and packaging processes. We also conduct regular tests for purity, consistency, and taste.

Do you offer organic rice options?

Yes, we have a line of certified organic rice products. These are grown without the use of synthetic pesticides or fertilizers and are processed separately to maintain their organic integrity.

Can I visit your rice mill?

We offer guided tours of our facility for educational purposes and for potential bulk buyers. Please contact us to schedule a visit.

Do you export your rice?

Yes, we export our rice to several countries. We comply with international food safety standards and can provide all necessary documentation for export.
