Our Rice Milling Process

From paddy to plate: The journey of premium rice

1. Harvesting

Our journey begins in the lush fields of Sheikhpura. We carefully monitor the rice crops and harvest at the optimal time to ensure peak flavor and nutritional value.

2. Drying and Storage

The harvested paddy is gently dried to the ideal moisture content. Our state-of-the-art storage facilities maintain perfect conditions to preserve quality.

3. Husking

Using advanced husking machines, we carefully remove the outer layer of the paddy, transforming it into brown rice while minimizing breakage.

4. Polishing

For our white rice varieties, we employ a precise polishing process that removes the bran layer while retaining essential nutrients.

5. Grading and Sorting

Our advanced optical sorting technology ensures that only the highest quality grains make it into our final products, removing any imperfections.

6. Packaging

The final step involves packaging our rice in high-quality, airtight packages to maintain freshness and extend shelf life.

Our Milling Journey

At Salman Rice Mill, we combine traditional techniques with modern technology to ensure the highest quality rice. Our process preserves the natural goodness of each grain while meeting the highest standards of cleanliness and efficiency.

Quality Control

Throughout our milling process, we maintain rigorous quality control measures:

  • Regular equipment calibration and maintenance
  • Strict hygiene protocols
  • Continuous monitoring of temperature and humidity
  • Frequent sampling and testing at each stage

These measures ensure that every grain of Salman rice meets our standards of excellence.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Salman Rice Mill, we're dedicated to sustainable practices:

  • Water conservation techniques in our fields
  • Use of rice husks as biofuel in our milling process
  • Minimizing waste through efficient processing
  • Supporting local farmers and communities
